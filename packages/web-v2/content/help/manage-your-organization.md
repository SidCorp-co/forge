# Manage your organization

An **organization** decides who can reach your projects. You always have a
**personal** org (just you); create a **team** org to work with others.

## Roles

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Everything, including renaming/deleting the org and managing owners. |
| **Admin** | Manage members and every project in the org. |
| **Member** | Belongs to the org; project access is granted per project. |

Org owners and admins automatically administer every project in the org. Plain
members only see projects they're explicitly added to.

## Create a team organization

1. Open **Settings → Organizations** and choose **Create organization**.
2. Give it a name and a short URL slug (lowercase letters, digits, hyphens).
3. You become its owner. Switch between orgs any time with the org switcher in
   the sidebar.

## Invite people

1. In **Settings → Organizations**, open your team org's **Members**.
2. Enter a teammate's email and pick a role.
   - If they already have a Forge account, they're added immediately.
   - If not, they receive an email invitation to join with that role.
3. Change a member's role or remove them from the same list. Pending email
   invitations can be revoked there too.

## Move a project between orgs

Open **Project → Settings → Advanced → Move to organization** and pick the
destination. You must be an owner/admin of **both** the current and the
destination org. The project's own members are kept.

## Notes

- An org must always keep at least one owner.
- A personal org can't be renamed, deleted, or given extra members.
- Deleting a team org requires moving or deleting its projects first.
